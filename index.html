Our Story

Quality Pedha Center is the most famous and trusted pedha shop in Ghargaon, proudly serving customers for over 50 years. Built on generations of trust and excellence, this family-owned sweet shop is lovingly run by Devidas Gadekar (Gadekar Bandhu).

For decades, we have been crafting authentic Indian sweets using pure milk, premium ingredients, and time-honored traditional recipes. We make and serve fresh pedhas every day, ensuring rich flavor, soft texture, and the homemade taste our customers love. More than just a sweet shop, Quality Pedha Center is a legacy of taste, tradition, and trust.
